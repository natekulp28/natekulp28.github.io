Nathaniel Kulp is a sophomore at Virginia Tech, pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering. He has hands-on experience in team leadership, robotics, and technical problem-solving through coursework and extracurricular projects. Nathaniel has actively contributed to Robotics Club, leading the development of competitive robots, and has a strong background in STEM-related volunteer work, including youth coaching and swim instruction.

Outside of academics, he enjoys competitive swimming, soccer, esports, and drones, earning multiple awards in high school athletics and robotics competitions. Nathaniel is seeking opportunities to apply his analytical abilities, technical knowledge, and collaborative skills through internships or entry-level positions in computer engineering.
